The poll questioned 1,786 registered voters on Nov. 9 and Nov. 10 about who would be their favorites for Trump to choose as White House advisers.Forty-eight percent of voters say Giuliani, while 46 percent chose Carson.New Jersey Gov. Chris Christie came in with 32 percent, while 23 percent chose Republican National Committee Chairman Reince Priebus.Twenty-two percent of voters said Sen. Jeff Sessions (R-AL) would be their favorite for an adviser position.The results have a two point margin of error.
